About the App

Breon is a minimal sleep app that guides your breathing using haptic feedback. No screens to watch, no sounds to listen to. Just hold your phone and breathe.

How It Works

  • Guided Phase — The app automatically guides your breathing with gentle haptic patterns. Inhale builds. Exhale fades. Just follow along.
  • Interactive Phase — Take control. Hold the left side of the screen to inhale, the right side to exhale. Haptics respond to your touch in real time.
  • Guided Mode — Enable in settings for fully hands-free haptic breathing. Close your eyes and let the vibrations guide you to sleep.
  • Breath Counter — Counts your breathing cycles like counting sheep. Enable or disable in settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

I don't feel any haptics.

Haptics only work on a physical iPhone (not in the simulator). Make sure your device supports haptics and check Settings → Sounds & Haptics → ensure "System Haptics" is enabled.

Which devices are supported?

Breon requires an iPhone with a haptic engine (iPhone 8 or later) running iOS 16.6 or newer. The app works without haptics on unsupported devices, but the core experience relies on them.

How do I adjust the screen brightness?

Tap the settings icon in the top-right corner during the breathing phase. Use the Interface Brightness slider to adjust how visible the UI elements are.

How do I reset the breath counter?

Open settings and tap "Reset Counter" below the Breath Counter toggle.

Is my data collected?

No. Breon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
