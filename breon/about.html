Breon

Fall asleep by feel.

A minimal sleep app that guides your breathing using haptic feedback. No screens to watch. No sounds to listen to. Just hold your phone, close your eyes, and breathe.

Haptic Breathing

Feel your breath. Inhale builds with rising intensity. Exhale fades with gentle release. Your phone becomes a breathing guide you can feel with your eyes closed.

Two Modes

Press and hold to breathe at your own pace, or enable Guided Mode for fully automatic haptic breathing. No interaction needed once you close your eyes.

Count Your Breaths

A subtle breath counter tracks your cycles like counting sheep. Watch the number grow as you drift off. Or disable it entirely.

Nothing Extra

No accounts. No analytics. No sounds. No ads. No subscriptions. No data collection. Just a dark screen, gentle vibrations, and sleep.
